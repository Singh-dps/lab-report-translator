# Product Overview

## Current status
MVP core implemented. Users can upload a lab report, get AI-powered plain-language interpretations, chat about results, and download a doctor summary PDF.

## Vision
A personal health OS that helps normal people understand lab reports and eventually connects biomarker interpretation with sleep, movement, nutrition, stress, and mood.

## Problem
Most lab reports in India are hard to understand — users get numbers, ranges, and medical jargon but not a clear picture of what matters or what to discuss with their doctor.

## What works today
- Upload a lab report (PDF or image) from a major Indian lab
- Get plain-language explanations for every value
- See concerning values highlighted with doctor questions
- Chat with an AI assistant about your specific report
- Download a one-page clinical summary PDF for doctor visits

## Supported labs
Dr Lal PathLabs, Thyrocare, Metropolis, SRL Diagnostics, Redcliffe Labs

## Target users
- Indian patients receiving routine blood work
- Health-conscious consumers who want explanations without jargon
- People preparing for doctor appointments

## UX principles
- Mobile-first, summary first, depth on demand
- Plain English with Indian cultural context
- Calm, trustworthy, medically responsible — never diagnostic
